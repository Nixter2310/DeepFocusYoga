YOGA

At different times of the year we offer different courses facilitated by very skilled teachers. Ranging from beginners yoga to breathwork and meditation, you’ll be sure to find something exciting to learn.

We offer a wide range of yoga practices, including:

  • Anusara
  • Iyengar
  • Bikram & Hot Yoga
  • Hatha (good for beginners!)
  • Ashtanga
  • Restorative
  • Vinyasa




Here’s a little preview of what to expect:


Classes run from Monday to Friday and usually last an hour (some classes can run 30-45 minutes depending on the type). You can book to be part of a group session, an private lesson or just you and a friend in ‘Buddy Yoga’.

Check out our prices:

  • Regular Yoga Class (group) - £10 per session or 5 sessions for £47
  • Private lesson (you and your tutor) - £25 per session
  • Buddy Yoga (you and a friend with a tutor) - £45 per session
