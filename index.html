ABOUT

Welcome to Deep Focus Yoga Studio.

We are a fully licensed studio based in Liverpool that specialises in yoga, zumba and wellbeing classes. We have a wide variety of sessions available to help you relax, unwind, get fit or just feel better about yourself.

Our fully qualified and experienced tutors can help you with your physical or mental needs, whether it's through a group session or a one-to-one consultation. We work around you.

We also have special guests visit the studio to give talks or teach classes, keep an eye on our social media to find out first and book your place in advance.

Please get in touch if you wish to know more about a particular class, have a free trial or would like to sign up. We promise to respond within 24 hours.

YOGA

Our yoga sessions cater for beginners and those who are more advanced. So no matter what age you are or what stage you're at we can find something for you. We teach Hatha yoga, Vinyasa yoga, Ashtanga yoga, Iyengar yoga, Kundalini yoga and more. We even host baby yoga classes on Saturday mornings!

WELLNESS

Our wellness programs can help you improve your mind and body. After an initial consultation we'll help you choose the program that will benefit you most. Programs include nutrition education, stress reduction, weight loss, smoking cessation and health screenings.
